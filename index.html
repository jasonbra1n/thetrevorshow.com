Meet Trevor LeBlanc

Trevor LeBlanc is Canada's #1 Interactive DJ and the visionary behind The Trevor Show. With a passion for entertainment spanning a lifetime, Trevor has evolved from singing with Basil from Blue Rodeo at age 9 to becoming one of North America's most sought-after entertainers.

As a dynamic blend of DJ, singer, dancer, and host, Trevor brings unparalleled energy to every event. His electrifying performances have graced television screens on Much Music and captivated audiences at prestigious festivals across the globe.

Recognized in the Magen Boys Entertainment Hall of Fame as Entertainer of the Year and featured on Canada's Got Talent 2023, Trevor continues to push the boundaries of interactive entertainment.
